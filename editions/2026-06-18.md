## A3: 🇨🇿 Czechia vs South Africa 🇿🇦
**Group A, MD2** | Thu June 18 — 12:00 PM ET (Fox) | Mercedes-Benz Stadium, Atlanta

**The Matchup:** South Africa's brave, Sundowns-styled identity — short high-tempo build-up from the back, aggressive man-to-man pressing — meets its most awkward stylistic counter. Czechia don't need to play through a press: they cycle laterally, go long and vertical to the wide areas, and win second balls in the opponent's half through Krejčí, Hranáč and Souček. A man-press is high-reward against teams that *must* build short; against a team happy to launch it toward Schick's head, it mostly creates 50/50s in dangerous areas.
