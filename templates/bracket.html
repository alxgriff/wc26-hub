How to read this page

If the group tables froze right now, who would meet whom — and how far would each side go? The draw is a lookup; the run beyond it is the model's best guess.

The draw (a lookup)

The 2026 bracket is fixed in advance — no post-group draw. Group winners, runners-up and the eight best third-placed teams drop into predetermined slots (matches 73–104), with the thirds assigned by the FIFA regulations' 495-row Annex C table. So who reaches the Round of 32 is settled, not forecast.

The run (a projection)

Beyond the R32, each tie is played out by the model: the projected advancer is shown in green with its chance to advance (90′ + extra time + a coin-flip shootout). Groups that haven't kicked off show abstract slots (Winner E, Best 3rd of …); $resolved_note The cascade only extends as far as both sides of a tie are known, and it all shifts with every result.
