## A1: 🇲🇽 Mexico vs South Africa 🇿🇦 — ✅ PLAYED: Mexico 2–0
**Group A, MD1** | Thu June 11 — 3:00 PM ET | Estadio Azteca, Mexico City

**The Matchup (as previewed):** The most vertical Mexico team in years — Aguirre's 4-3-3 stretching into a 4-1-4-1 with industrious pressing midfielders — against South Africa's Sundowns-style short-passing build-up and aggressive man-to-man press. **The Matchup:** Two back-three teams with opposite intentions. Koubek's Czechia run a 3-4-3 that becomes a 4-1-5, cycling the ball laterally in the back line and going vertical to the wide areas, where multiple proficient crossers and a refined set-piece operation feed Patrik Schick's elite aerial threat. The catch for Korea: the whole system runs through Hwang In-beom's spatial intelligence, and he's recovering from an ankle injury. The catch for Czechia: if Korea's technical midfield (Lee Kang-in, Paik) controls tempo, Czechia's strength — winning second balls in the opponent's half via Krejčí, Hranáč and Souček — never activates.

**Group C, MD1** | Sat June 13 — 6:00 PM ET (FS1) | MetLife Stadium, East Rutherford
