Ismaël Koné versus Sofyan Amrabat. Koné — whose breakout season at Sassuolo has made him indispensable to Marsch's system — carries the pressing workrate and ball-progression that makes Canada's midfield function as a unit. Amrabat anchors Morocco's defensive midfield and is the primary barrier between Canada's transition moments and the Atlas Lions' more expansive attackers. If Amrabat holds shape and controls tempo, Morocco's 3-2-5 possession structure expands and Canada are left chasing.
