Tactical preview

Group J, MD2 | Mon June 22 — 1:00 PM ET (Fox) | AT&T Stadium, Arlington

The best tactical clash of MD2: Rangnick's maximal press against the most press-resistant midfield on Earth. Austria will hunt Argentina's build-up with Seiwald, Schlager and Laimer flying at Paredes' pivot — and Argentina's entire post-2022 evolution (Enzo, Mac Allister, De Paul/Palacios rotating) is engineered to play through exactly this and punish the space behind it. If Argentina escape the first wave cleanly twice, Messi and Alvarez are running at a stretched back line with Alaba's recovery legs as the last line. If Austria's press lands — and it has landed on elite teams — the champions' flagged fitness issues (Paredes, Alvarez, Martínez) become the story. No MD2 game has a wider range of plausible outcomes.

Key Duel

Austria's first pressing wave vs Leandro Paredes' first touch — the entire philosophical argument in one repeated moment.

Watch For

Wanner in the Baumgartner role against the champions. Messi's biggest group-stage test. Nico Paz if Scaloni wants extra press resistance.
